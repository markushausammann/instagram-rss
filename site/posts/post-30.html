Day 6 of 6 hiking in Swedish Lapland. Just 14km left to walk, exhausted but happy. The views were still varied and beautiful, but it was kind of sad to say goodbye to the mountains and almost overwhelming to see so many hikers. I really like it when you can walk for days without meeting a soul. This has definitely been one of the best adventures of my life so far and I can't wait to get back for another project.
