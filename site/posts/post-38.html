Jul 24, 2023 11:09 PM

Yesterday, day 1 of our 6 day adventure in Swedish Lapland. We arrived in Kolari by car train at 10, drove to Kiruna for last food shopping and then to Nikkaluokta. From there we took a path through a lush forest up unto a rarely visited high plateau. Despite the clouds we were treated with amazing views. The camp was wet and cold. 5 degrees in the night, but warm coffee and porridge in the morning helps.
